# Safety Policy

## Important Disclaimers

### Generated Content

All procedures generated by Method.AI are **DRAFT DOCUMENTS** intended for review and adaptation by qualified professionals. They are NOT:

- Verified experimental protocols
- Validated for safety or efficacy
- Guaranteed to be correct or complete
- Suitable for direct execution without review

### User Responsibilities

Users of this software are solely responsible for:

1. **Verification**: Thoroughly reviewing all generated content
2. **Safety Assessment**: Conducting proper risk assessments
3. **Compliance**: Ensuring adherence to all applicable regulations
4. **Expertise**: Having appropriate qualifications and training
5. **Institutional Approval**: Obtaining necessary permissions
6. **Supervision**: Ensuring appropriate oversight of all work

### No Warranties

This software is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. The authors and contributors explicitly disclaim liability for any outcomes resulting from use of generated content.

## Design Boundaries

### What This System Does

- Generates generic procedural frameworks
- Provides structural templates for common workflows
- Flags potential constraints based on lab context
- Suggests alternative approaches at a high level
- Records feedback for improvement

### What This System Does NOT Do

- Generate specific instructions for controlled substances
- Provide detailed protocols for hazardous processes
- Guarantee safety or regulatory compliance
- Replace professional judgment or expertise
- Serve as a primary source for experimental procedures

## Content Guidelines

### Generated Procedures

All generated procedures:

1. Use generic terminology where possible
2. Include prominent disclaimers
3. Reference need for professional review
4. Avoid specific quantities for sensitive materials
5. Emphasize safety verification requirements

### Risk Flags

Risk annotations are informational only and:

- May not capture all relevant concerns
- Should not replace formal risk assessment
- Are based on limited context information
- Require professional interpretation

## Intended Use

This software is intended for:

- Qualified professionals in appropriate settings
- Educational and research contexts
- Workflow planning and organization
- Generating starting points for protocol development

This software is NOT intended for:

- Direct execution without professional review
- Use by unqualified individuals
- Circumventing safety procedures
- Generating content for harmful purposes

## Reporting Concerns

If you identify safety concerns with the software or generated content, please report them immediately via our security reporting process.
